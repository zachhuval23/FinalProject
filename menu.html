Mountain Menu of the Day

Hot Dog

Our hotdogs are crafted with the finest quality ingredients, starting with juicy, all-beef franks that burst with flavor in every bite. Nestled within a soft, perfectly toasted bun, our hotdogs are a symphony of textures and tastes. Each bite offers a delightful combination of savory and smoky flavors, creating a truly satisfying experience.

Hamburger

At our establishment, we start with only the finest quality ingredients. Our burgers feature premium, hand-selected beef patties, seasoned to perfection and cooked to juicy, flavorful perfection. Each bite is a symphony of tenderness, with the meat boasting a satisfying char and an irresistible smoky aroma.

French Fries

Our French fries are made from the finest quality potatoes, hand-cut to the ideal thickness. They are then expertly cooked to achieve the perfect balance of a golden, crispy exterior and a fluffy, tender interior. With each bite, you'll experience a satisfying crunch followed by a burst of fluffy potato goodness that will leave you yearning for another fry.
